About the Game

Welcome to HOLOCURE! This game lets you collect powerful weapons and items, experiment with various builds, and create the ultimate character to defeat endless waves of enemies and reach the top rankings. It's an engaging experience for fans of rogue-lite action.

Key Features

  • Unlock and play with 47 Hololive characters through Gacha (in-game currency only).
  • Unique skills and special attacks for every character.
  • Unlock dozens of different weapons and passive items.
  • Hololive song remixes created for this game by Eufrik.
  • Survive against classic Hololive characters and mascots.
  • Available in English, Japanese, and Indonesian languages.
